Used-Car Sales Analysis for Market Expansion

Leveraging Data Analytics for Strategic Growth

To support the expansion into Mississauga, we analyzed historical sales data from our Toronto branch using SPSS Modeler and Cognos to identify key market trends, optimize pricing, and predict sales volume. By leveraging Decision Trees and Linear Regression, we determined that price, resale value, engine size, horsepower, curb weight, fuel capacity, and fuel efficiency were the primary drivers of sales. Our data-backed approach enables strategic pricing and inventory management, ensuring profitability and customer satisfaction in the new location.
